ESIC offers comprehensive academic programs. Each pathway is designed with a clear progression to university admission.

Preparatory Course for Singapore-Cambridge Secondary Education Certificate (SEC)

The Preparatory Course for Singapore-Cambridge Secondary Education Certificate (SEC) program provides students with comprehensive preparation for the sit for the new Singapore-Cambridge Secondary Education Certificate (SEC) examinations from 2027 onwards

  • Duration: 24 Months Full-Time (5 days per week, 6 hours per day)
  • Age Requirement: 15 years
  • Subjects: 5-8 subjects based on the Compulsory and Selective subject
  • Mode of Study: Face-to-face
View Details
Preparatory Course for Singapore-Cambridge General Certificate of Education (Ordinary Level) (Intensive)

The Preparatory Course for Singapore-Cambridge General Certificate of Education (Ordinary Level) (Intensive) program provides students with comprehensive preparation for the Singapore-Cambridge GCE O-Level examinations

  • Duration: 12 Months Full-Time (5 days per week, 6 hours per day)
  • Age Requirement: 16+ years
  • Subjects: 5-8 subjects based on the Compulsory and Selective subject
  • Mode of Study: Face-to-face
View Details
Preparatory Course for Singapore-Cambridge General Certificate of Education (Advanced Level)

The Preparatory Course for Singapore-Cambridge General Certificate of Education (Advanced Level) program provides students with comprehensive preparation for the sit for the Singapore-Cambridge General Certificate of Education (Advanced Level) examination.

  • Duration: 24 Months Full-Time (5 days per week, 6 hours per day)
  • Age Requirement: 16+ years
  • Subjects: Students will choose 3H2 +1H1+GP or 4H2+GP based on the compulsory and selective Module.
  • Mode of Study: Face-to-face
View Details
Preparatory Course for Pearson Edexcel International Advanced Level

The Preparatory Course for Pearson Edexcel International Advanced Level program prepares students for the Pearson Edexcel International A-Levels (IAL) examination.

  • Duration: 24 Months Full-Time (5 days per week, 6 hours per day)
  • Age Requirement: 16+ years
  • Subjects: Students will select 2 compulsory subjects and at least 2 to 4 optional subjects to study
  • Mode of Study: Face-to-face
