About Us

Established in 2010, Equistar International College (ESIC) has built a strong academic framework supported by robust systems and processes. With nearly 15 years of experience in Singapore, the College is committed to delivering quality education that integrates academic rigor, industry relevance, and holistic student development, empowering learners to achieve their aspirations and excel in a globalized world.

Our Academic Programs

Preparatory Course for Singapore-Cambridge Secondary Education Certificate (SEC)
  • Program Duration: 24 months Full-Time (5 days per week, 6 hours per day)
  • Age: 15 years old
  • Course Modules: Students will choose 5-8 subjects based on the Compulsory and Selective subject. The students taken subject should include English, Mathematics, Science and Humanity subject.
View Details
Preparatory Course for Singapore-Cambridge General Certificate of Education (Ordinary Level) (Intensive)
  • Program Duration: 12 months Full-Time (5 days per week, 6 hours per day)
  • Age: 16 years old
  • Course Modules: Students will choose 5-8 subjects based on the Compulsory and Selective Modules.
View Details
Preparatory Course for Singapore-Cambridge General Certificate of Education (Advanced Level)
  • Program Duration: 24 months Full-Time (5 days per week, 6 hours per day)
  • Age: 16 years old
  • Course Modules: Students will choose 3H2 +1H1+GP or 4H2+GP based on the compulsory and selective Module.

Preparatory Course for Pearson Edexcel International Advanced Level
  • Program Duration: 24 months Full-Time (5 days per week, 6 hours per day)
  • Age: 16 years old
  • Course Modules: Students will select 2 compulsory subjects and at least 2 to 4 optional subjects to study.
