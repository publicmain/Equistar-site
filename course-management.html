Course Overview

Get a comprehensive understanding of the business world through our PG Diploma. The engaging curriculum covers major business verticals and equips you with practical knowledge and skills for success in any industry.

There are no planned intakes for this course.

Key Highlights
  • PG Diploma from Equistar International College
  • 360° Career Support & 1:1 Career Coaching
  • 200+ Hours of learning & 25+ Live Sessions
  • Live sessions by global experts
  • Support available all days
  • AI Profile Builder
  • Industry-driven projects & case studies
  • Harvard Business Review simulations
3 Reasons to pursue this program
  1. Enhanced Skills & Career Advancement – build managerial excellence, tackle complex challenges, and attract employer attention.
  2. Access to Global Subject Matter Experts – learn the latest insights and best practices for leadership roles.
  3. Networking Opportunities – connect with experts and alumni for mentorship and partnerships.
Certificate

Post Graduate Diploma in Management (E-Learning) from Equistar International College.

  • Be part of the international community
  • Network with like-minded individuals and learners
  • Access our global subject-matter experts
  • Enhance your management skills
Top Topics You Will Learn

Business Growth & Marketing Strategies; Leadership & Teamwork; Structured Thinking & Problem-solving; Adaptability to Change; Interpersonal & Communication Skills; Strategic Planning; Time & Project Management.

Graduation Requirements
  • Complete the course.
  • Pass each module with at least a C- grade (≥40%).
  • Learners failing to score ≥40% in more than 3 modules will not be eligible for the PGD Mgt certificate.
  • Assessment via Assignments, Projects, and Written Examinations.
Who Is This Course For?

Domain experts; aspiring or current general managers and entrepreneurs seeking an in-depth understanding of management and leadership; IT professionals looking to switch industry.

Minimum Eligibility
  • Minimum Age: 21 years
  • Academic: Bachelor's degree in any discipline with a minimum of 50% (or GPA ≥2.0/4.0); or 3 years relevant work experience, including ≥2 years (full-time equivalent) in managerial/professional roles involving decision-making (budgetary oversight, staffing/management, strategy & planning, operational implementation).
  • Language: Bachelor's degree delivered in English or IELTS 6.0 (or equivalent).
Syllabus
  • Teacher–Student Ratio: 1:20
  • Hours of Learning: 200+
  • Hours of Live Sessions: 25+
  • Course Duration: 8 months (full-time)
  • Content delivered through videos, case studies, projects, and assignments.

The course shall be conducted fully through online delivery.

Marketing Management
  • Create customer value through effective positioning of products and services.
  • Understand the consumer behaviour buying model and the Consumer Decision-Making Process to deliver value with the right marketing mix.
  • Project.
Accounting and Finance
  • Understand fundamental accounting terms and how business events impact financial statements.
  • Analyse profitability and manage working capital using financial statements.
  • Evaluate projects using investment appraisal metrics and decide which projects to pursue.
  • Project.
Business Economics
Business Communication
  • Apply verbal and non-verbal communication techniques to communicate effectively at work.
  • Leverage appropriate communication channels to deliver your message.
  • Create a personal-branding vision statement; use branding assets and monitoring techniques for your brand and competition.
  • Project.
Decision Sciences
  • Identify suitable metrics in business situations to communicate requirements with data scientists.
  • Build and test hypotheses from small samples to drive business decisions.
  • Predict future trends using existing data (e.g., linear regression).
  • Project.
OB, HR and Legal
  • Build strong teams by understanding individual behaviour, group dynamics and organisational culture.
  • Manage key HR responsibilities to build and maintain effective teams as a line manager.
  • Become literate in essential legal terminology to make informed business decisions.
  • Project.
Operations and Supply Chain
  • Identify demand patterns and plan supply under organisational constraints at optimal cost.
  • Understand sourcing across the product cycle; steps for evaluating and selecting suppliers.
  • Learn logistics & transportation management and design elements of a distribution network.
  • Project.
Sales and Distribution
  • Understand frameworks for effective selling.
  • Learn channel-partner types and parameters for selecting, onboarding and evaluating partners.
  • Create and manage sales territories and analyse territory performance.
  • Project.
Business Strategy
  • Analyse how macro- and micro-environmental factors influence firm operations and strategy.
  • Apply growth strategies to attain market leadership and sustain industry growth.
  • Project.
