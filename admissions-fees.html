Fees & Payments

Transparent fee structure and payment policies

Fee Protection Scheme (FPS)

The FPS serves to protect the students' fees in the event that a PEI is unable to continue operating due to insolvency, and/or regulatory closure. The FPS also protects the students if the PEI fails to pay penalties or return fees to the students arising from judgments made against it by the Singapore courts.

The FPS is compulsory for all students taking programmes at PEIs seeking EduTrust certification.

At Equistar International College, we have entered into an agreement with Lonpac Insurance for our FPS requirements. All course fees will be protected under the Lonpac Insurance.

Kindly refer to SSG website for more details.

Student Contract & Cooling-Off Period
  • 10-Day Cooling-Off Period: 100% refund if contract cancelled within 10 calendar days of signing.
  • Contract Explanation: ESIC staff will explain course details, fees, refund terms, and FPS before signing.

Important: Students and guardians (for minors) must fully understand the contract terms before signing. All questions should be clarified during the contract explanation session.

Late Payment Policy

If payment is not received within one day after the stipulated deadline as per Student Contract, the Registrar and Student Support Services Department will send a reminder to the Student informing them of the 1-month late payment policy.

The College does not charge a late payment fee. However, should payment remain outstanding, the Student may be withdrawn from the College at the discretion of the College's Management Team.
