With a constantly growing market, AI is in every industry, top businesses and across functions. From supply chain to business development, business analytics, digital marketing, and more. AI today open up more opportunities and career options than ever before. Champion the most in demand skills and be armed with the skills of the future.

Certificate

Post Graduate Certificate in Generative AI (E-Learning)

Upon successful completion of the Programme, you will receive a certificate for Post Graduate Certificate in Generative AI (E-Learning) course from Equistar International College.

  • Become the most desirable candidate for the most in-demand jobs that require ChaptGPT, Prompt Engineering, Generative AI Skills and more
  • Learn to develop and deploy Generative AI apps like OpenAI, Midjourney, DALL-E and more
  • Unlock Equistar International College Alumni Status
Top Skills you Will Learn from our Generative AI Course

Generative AI Models, Transformers, ChatGPT, Prompt Engineering, Product Development, Deploy Web Apps with Flask

Graduation Requirements

A Post Graduate Certificate in Generative AI (E-Learning) will be awarded to students who fulfill:

  • Completion of the course
  • A learner will have to secure a minimum of 20% in any 4 projects out of 6.

Students will be assessed based on Assignments, Projects and Written Exams.

Who Is This Course For?

Engineers, Software and IT Professionals, Data Professionals, Learners from non-technical backgrounds who want to excel in the Gen AI domain.

Minimum Eligibility
  • Minimum Age: 21 Years Old
  • Language Proficiency: Bachelor's Degree where English is the mode of delivery, or IELTS 6.0, or equivalent.
  • Academic Level: Bachelor Degree (or equivalent); or mature candidates who are at least 30 years old and above with 8 years of work experience and with a Diploma; and with basic programming proficiency in any language.
Syllabus
  • Teacher–Student Ratio: 1:20
  • Hours of Learning: 180+
  • Hours of Live Sessions: 70+
  • Course Duration: 6 months (full-time)
  • Content delivered through videos, case studies, projects, and assignments.

The course shall be conducted fully through online delivery.

Core Modules
  • Programming 101
  • Create ShopAssistAI
  • Create PixxelCraft AI
  • Create ShrewdNews AI
  • Create Mr.HelpMate AI
  • SemanticSpotter - Mr.HelpMate AI on Steroids
  • Scale Mr.HelpMate AI using Azure OpenAI services
  • Future Developments in Generative AI
Course Fees
  • Course Fee: SGD $ 3,000
  • Application Fee: SGD $ 260 (non-refundable)
  • Deferment fees are at 10% of the course fee and are non-refundable
